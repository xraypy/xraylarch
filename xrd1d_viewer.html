7. Larch XRD1D ViewerÂ¶

The Larch XRD1D application allows simple viewing and manipulation of 1-dimensional XRD patterns of diffracted intensity as a function of scattering wavenumber or angle: \(I(q)\), \(I(2\theta)\). The application allows

  1. reading and comparison of multiple XRD patterns.

  2. fitting and subtracting a background from an XRD pattern.
